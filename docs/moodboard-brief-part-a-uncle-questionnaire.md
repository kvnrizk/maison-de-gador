# La Maison de Gador — Brand Discovery
## Part A — Questions for the Founder (Uncle)

*To be answered before the moodboard phase. The more concrete and personal the answers, the stronger the brand. One- or two-line answers are enough for each — full paragraphs welcome if you feel inspired.*

---

### 1. The Soul of the Brand

1.1 **In one sentence, what is La Maison de Gador to you?**
   *(Not the product description — the feeling. What should someone remember after biting one?)*

1.2 **Where does the name "Gador" come from, in the family story?**
   *(We'll use this as a narrative anchor across website, packaging, social.)*

1.3 **What three words must people associate with the brand?**
   *(e.g. "generosity, craft, quiet luxury" — or your own words.)*

1.4 **What three words must people *never* associate with the brand?**
   *(e.g. "gaudy, mass-market, cliché". Helps us filter design choices.)*

1.5 **What is the emotion you want the customer to feel when they open the box?**

---

### 2. The Product

2.1 **How many distinct products will we offer at launch?**
   *(Exact SKUs: flavours, fillings, toppings. Even a rough list helps.)*

2.2 **For each, what are the exact ingredients and origin?**
   - Date variety and origin (Medjool from where specifically?)
   - Chocolate origin (Belgian? Single-origin cacao? Brand of couverture?)
   - Fillings, nuts, gold leaf, edible finishes
   - Any halal / organic / single-origin certifications

2.3 **Price points per piece and per box** (QAR and USD, both retail and corporate):
   - Entry box (smallest gift)
   - Mid-tier box
   - Premium / flagship box
   - Per-piece pricing for loose selection

2.4 **Shelf life, storage, and shipping constraints.**
   *(Hot Qatar climate vs. Belgian chocolate — how we handle this defines the channel strategy.)*

2.5 **Are the products already finalised, or are we still iterating?**
   *(Be honest — affects whether the moodboard can lock visual direction now.)*

2.6 **Can you send me 3–5 high-res photos of the actual product** (even on a plain white background with phone camera)? We cannot moodboard without seeing the real thing.

---

### 3. The Client

3.1 **Describe the ONE ideal customer** in detail. Give them a name, age, nationality, neighbourhood, profession, what they drive, where they holiday, what they give as gifts.
   *(Not market segments — one real human.)*

3.2 **What occasion are they buying for?**
   Rank the top 3 from:
   - Ramadan / Eid gifting
   - Weddings / engagements
   - Corporate / B2B gifting
   - Hospitality (hosting at home)
   - Self-indulgence
   - Mother's Day / personal gifting
   - Other: _____

3.3 **Will we sell primarily to individuals or to businesses (hotels, offices, event planners)?**
   *(Shifts everything: packaging formats, pricing logic, channel.)*

3.4 **Geography:** Qatar only at launch? Which neighbourhoods first? Expansion window to Beirut / UAE / KSA?

3.5 **Language priority:** Arabic-first, English-first, or perfectly bilingual?

---

### 4. Competitors & References

4.1 **Which 3 brands — in any category (not just chocolate) — do you admire and want to feel like?**
   *(Hermès, Dior, Bateel, Patchi, Aesop, La Maison du Chocolat, Apple, a local Qatari boutique… be specific.)*

4.2 **Which 3 brands do you explicitly NOT want to be confused with?**
   *(Equally important.)*

4.3 **Who is the biggest direct competitor in Qatar right now, in your opinion?**
   *(Bateel? Patchi? Forrey & Galland? Someone local?)*

4.4 **What do they do well? What do they do badly? What gap are we filling?**

---

### 5. Brand Pillars & Storytelling

5.1 **Why chocolate dates specifically** — and not chocolate alone, or dates alone, or pastries?
   *(The answer becomes our core narrative.)*

5.2 **Is there a cultural or religious framing you want to honour explicitly?**
   *(Gulf hospitality, Islamic gifting tradition, family heritage, etc.)*

5.3 **What's the story of the phoenix emblem?** Who designed it and what does it mean to you?

5.4 **Is there an anecdote, memory, or tradition you'd like embedded into the brand identity?**
   *(Often the strongest branding elements come from a single specific memory.)*

---

### 6. Commercial & Operational

6.1 **Launch timeline:** target unveiling date? Soft launch / invite-only / full public?

6.2 **Legal status:** where are we in Qatar business registration? What's the realistic date we can accept payments?

6.3 **Retail presence:** will we have a physical boutique, be sold through third-party retailers (hotels, gift shops), or online + WhatsApp only at launch?

6.4 **Budget range for brand identity, packaging, and launch assets?**
   *(Rough order of magnitude — changes what's realistic.)*

6.5 **Who else is involved in the decision?** (Any family members or partners whose opinion needs to be aligned before we move forward.)

---

### 7. Personal

7.1 **What would make you proud to show this brand to a close friend?**

7.2 **What would embarrass you?**

7.3 **Three years from now, how do you want this brand described in a newspaper article?**

---

*Please answer in writing (voice note transcribed is fine too). Even rough answers unlock everything on my side.*
