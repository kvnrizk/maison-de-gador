### Logo Construction
- "La Maison De" in flowing script/calligraphic style above
- "GADOR" in bold serif with custom letter styling — the O contains a distinctive cocoa bean/date shape
- "— Chocolatier —" in spaced small caps below, flanked by decorative dashes
- Phoenix icon rendered in gold, positioned above or beside the wordmark

## Icon (Phoenix)
- Stylized phoenix/bird with spread wings
- Flame element rising from the top
- Rendered in gold (#C9A84E) on jewel-tone backgrounds
- Used as favicon, profile picture, standalone mark

## Illustration Style

Opulent Middle Eastern luxury aesthetic. Gold metallic elements on jewel-tone backgrounds (teal, burgundy). Phoenix motifs and flame imagery. No botanical line art — instead, rich textures, geometric patterns, and gilded accents. Used as decorative elements, never as primary content.

## Brand Voice
- "The Art of Adoration" / "فن التذوق"
- French luxury meets Gulf heritage
- Premium but warm, not cold luxury
- Artisan, handcrafted, attention to detail
