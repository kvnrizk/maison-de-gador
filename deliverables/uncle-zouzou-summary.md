# La Maison de Gador — Project Summary
## For Uncle Zouzou

---

## What We Are Building

We are creating a complete digital presence for **La Maison de Gador** — your premium chocolate dates brand in Qatar.

Here is what you will get:

---

## 1. Professional Website

A modern, elegant e-commerce website where customers can:

- Browse your date collections and gift boxes
- Place orders online and pay securely (using **Dibsy**, a Qatar-approved payment system)
- Request custom orders and corporate gifts
- Contact you directly via WhatsApp

The website will be **bilingual** (Arabic + English) and optimized for mobile phones.

**Your role**: Provide product photos, descriptions, and approve the final design.

---

## 2. Social Media Pages

We will create and manage:

- **Instagram** business page
- **Facebook** business page

### What we will post:
- Beautiful product photos
- Short videos (Reels) showing your dates and gift boxes
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Seasonal campaigns (Ramadan, Eid, National Day)
- Customer testimonials

### How it works:
- We prepare all content in advance (photos, captions, scheduling)
- **You approve each post** before it goes live (via a WhatsApp group)
- Posts are scheduled automatically using Meta Business Suite (free tool from Facebook/Instagram)

### Posting schedule:
- 4-5 posts per week on Instagram and Facebook
- Daily Stories
- 3 video Reels per week

---

## 3. Advertising & Growth

- We will boost your best posts on Instagram and Facebook
- Recommended starting budget: **$5-10 per day** ($150-300 per month)
- Targeting: People in Doha interested in food, dining, gifts, and luxury lifestyle
- We will collaborate with Qatar food influencers to spread the word

---
